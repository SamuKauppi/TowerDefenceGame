Enemy
- Finds a way from start to end
- Dies and does damage to player when reaches the end
- Has hp and takes damage from towers
- Different types
Tower
- Shoots enemy when in range
- Has hp and takes damage from specific enemies
- Has the ability to be upgraded into any path
Player
- Gains money every X second
- Can spend money to buy or upgrade towers. Towers can be placed anywhere if there is room for one
- Has hp and takes damage if an enemy goes to end. Game ends when hp is 0
Game
- Spawns different enemies based on players towers
- Has a budget to spawn enemies which increases over time.
